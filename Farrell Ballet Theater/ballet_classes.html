Ballet Classes

Adult Ballet Classes

Adult classes are offered year round and students may start at any time. The start times for Kalamazoo college students coincide with the academic calendar. FBT specializes in ballet classes for adults – students range in age from 4 to 70.

To Register for ballet classes:

Phone Sher Marie Farrell, 269-381-1058
Studio is located on the campus of Kalamazoo College.
1200 Academy St., Anderson Athletic Center, Lower Level.

Adult Beginning Ballet
Monday/Wednesday, 10:00 am to 11:35 am

Adult Intermediate Ballet
Monday/Wednesday, 11:50 am to 1:05 pm

Company Technique
Tuesday/Thursday, 6:00 pm to 7:45 pm

Tuition

Tuition is based on an annual amount from September to June, payable the first week of each month.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------ ----------------------
One class per week $45 per month
Two classes per week $90 per month
Three classes per week $135 per month
Open class (Fall, Winter, and Spring) $12 per class
Open class (Summer) $15 per class
------------------------------------------------------------------------------ ----------------------

Class Attire

Black leotard, pink tights, pink slippers.
Pointe shoes with permission from instructor
